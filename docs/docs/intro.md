# Introduction to AI Robotics Book

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** book - a comprehensive guide to bridging the gap between digital AI systems and physical robotic platforms.

## About This Book

This book explores the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics, focusing on how AI systems can be applied to control Humanoid Robots in both simulated and real-world environments. We call this domain "Embodied Intelligence".

### What You'll Learn

- How AI systems operate in physical environments
- Techniques for controlling humanoid robots
- Simulated and real-world robotics applications
- Embodied intelligence principles and implementations
- Advanced sensorimotor learning paradigms
