## Focus and Theme: AI Systems in the Physical World

Welcome to the fascinating frontier where artificial intelligence meets the physical realm. This book explores Embodied Intelligence - the practice of applying AI knowledge to control Humanoid Robots in simulated and real-world environments. Our mission is to bridge the gap between the digital brain and the physical body.

### What You'll Discover

- How AI systems operate in physical environments
- Techniques for controlling humanoid robots
- Simulated and real-world robotics applications
- Embodied intelligence principles and implementations
- Advanced sensorimotor learning paradigms

## About This Course

This comprehensive program focuses on applying your existing AI knowledge to control Humanoid Robots in both simulated and real-world environments. Through hands-on projects, theoretical foundations, and practical implementations, you'll gain expertise in Embodied Intelligence - the discipline concerned with AI systems that interact with the physical world.

Whether you're a student, researcher, or industry professional, this course will provide the knowledge and tools you need to develop and deploy AI systems in the physical realm.
